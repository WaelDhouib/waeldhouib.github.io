About Me

I’m a mobile developer specializing in Flutter, currently working at Wind Consulting in Tunisia. With a solid foundation in computer science and a focus on data science, I develop mobile applications that combine clean UI design, seamless API integration, and performance optimization. My experience includes building internal tools like Wind PDA, an Android inventory management app integrated with ERP systems, as well as contributing to Wind ERP Mobile and Autopal. I also worked on the design and user experience of ESS Mobile, a project that highlights my attention to usability and technical detail.

I’m passionate about creating impactful solutions in collaborative, dynamic teams and seeking opportunities in France or Europe to further my career. My engineering background equips me to handle structured data and backend logic for robust, scalable applications. I speak Arabic, French, and English, and bring adaptability, technical expertise, and cultural versatility to every project.
